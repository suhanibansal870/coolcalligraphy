About Us


Cool Calligraphy is a website focused on teaching the basics of old and modern calligraphy. This website makes learning calligraphy easy and fun. We have tutorials and instructions to help you, if you've never used a brush pen before and want to learn how. .

What is Calligraphy?


Calligraphy is an art related to writing. It is the design of lettering with a pen or ink brush. There are two different kinds of calligraphy; Modern Calligraphy and Old Calligraphy.
